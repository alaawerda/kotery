How to Delete Your User Information in Splanit

Last updated:
This guide explains how to permanently delete all your personal information from the Splanit app, step by step.
Step 1: Access the Account Deletion Page
  1. Open the app and log in to your account.
  2. Go to the Settings or Profile menu.
  3. Click on the Delete My Account or Delete My Data option.
Step 2: Confirm Deletion
  1. Read the warning about the permanent deletion of your data carefully.
  2. Tick the confirmation checkbox if required.
  3. Type DELETE MY DATA in the confirmation field to proceed.
  4. Enter your account password to confirm your identity.
  5. Click the Continue button.
Step 3: Final Verification
  1. A confirmation message will appear indicating that your account and data have been deleted.
  2. You will be automatically logged out of the app.
